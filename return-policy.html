Return & Refund Policy

Thank you for shopping with us. We take pride in offering high-quality suits and want you to be fully satisfied with your purchase. Please read our return policy carefully.

1. Returns Eligibility

We accept returns under the following conditions:

  • Items must be returned within 7-14 days of delivery.
  • The suit must be unused, unworn, and in original condition.
  • All original tags, packaging, and accessories must be included.
  • Items that are damaged, altered, or show signs of wear will not be accepted.

2. Non-Returnable Items

The following items cannot be returned:

  • Custom-made or tailored suits
  • Sale or discounted items (unless defective)
  • Gift cards or promotional items

3. Exchange Policy

We offer exchanges for:

  • Incorrect sizes
  • Defective or damaged items upon delivery

If you need a different size, please contact us and we will assist with an exchange based on availability.

4. Refund Process

  • Once your return is received and inspected, we will notify you of approval or rejection.
  • If approved, your refund will be processed within 5-10 business days.
  • Refunds will be issued using the original payment method.

5. Return Shipping

  • Customers are responsible for return shipping costs unless the item was defective or incorrect.
  • We recommend using a trackable shipping service to ensure your return is received.

6. Damaged or Incorrect Items

If you receive a defective or wrong item:

  • Contact us within 48 hours of delivery.
  • Provide clear photos of the issue.
  • We will arrange a replacement or full refund at no extra cost.

7. How to Initiate a Return

To start a return, please contact us via:

  • Email: cse25-296@thuto.bac.ac.bw

Include your order number and reason for return.

Note: By purchasing from our website, you agree to this return policy.

If you have any questions, feel free to contact our support team.
